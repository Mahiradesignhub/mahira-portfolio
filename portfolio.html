Thesis

In this thesis, Mahira skillfully combines words and illustrations to reflect the complexities of contemporary society in Lahore. With a creative yet thoughtful approach, she has specifically crafted these books for children, aiming to nurture values and awareness from an early age. Her objective is to inspire young minds to grow into responsible individuals who can contribute positively to the future of this country. Using the character of Shaitaan as a symbolic figure struggling between right and wrong, the work presents moral dilemmas in an engaging way for young readers. This thesis offers an insightful exploration of these themes.Let's dive deeper into it shall we?
